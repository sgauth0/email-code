**Pinned Accounts** (Daily Drivers)

## üéØ Three Instant Scopes

**0** - All accounts unified (everything, everywhere)
**9** - Favorites only (pinned accounts unified)

## üî§ Navigation

### Account Switching
- **[** / **]** - Cycle through pinned accounts only
- **0** - Switch to All (unified across everything)
- **9** - Switch to Favorites (unified across pinned only)
- **1-8** - Jump directly to pinned account 1-8
- **g g** - Open "More..." account picker

### Focus Zones
- **Tab** - Cycle forward (folders ‚Üí threads ‚Üí detail)
- **Shift+Tab** - Cycle backward
- **j** / **k** - Context-aware navigation (up/down in current zone)

### Folder Navigation (when sidebar focused)
- **i** - Jump to Inbox
- **a** - Jump to Archive
- **d** - Jump to Drafts
- **t** - Jump to Trash

## üìß Thread Actions

- **Enter** - Open thread (in threads) / Archive & next (in detail)
- **Space** - Toggle read/unread status
- **e** - Archive thread
- **d** - Delete thread
- **m** - Open move-to-folder picker
- **s** - Star/unstar thread

## üé® Account Picker (More...)

**Access:** Click "More..." or press **g g**

**Navigation:**
- **j** / **k** - Move through accounts
- **Enter** - Show selected account
- **p** - Toggle pin/unpin
- **f** - Toggle favorites inclusion
- **Escape** - Close picker

**Features:**
- Health indicators (green/yellow/red dots)
- Pin/unpin any account
- Include/exclude from favorites
- Show any account temporarily
